# P3 — v0.3.0 Progression Alpha

## Objective

Visualize consistency without creating shame, compulsion or reward-first behavior.

## Features

- Disciplina calculation policy.
- Rank progression.
- Camp growth visualization.
- Cosmetic equipment unlocks.
- Formation Integrity instead of destructive streak reset.
- Recovery Days.
- Progress explanation and audit trail.

## Guardrails

- No loot boxes, random paid rewards or loss aversion timers.
- Missed days never erase accumulated work.
- Progression is derived only from accepted focus records.
- The mission remains visually dominant over progression.
