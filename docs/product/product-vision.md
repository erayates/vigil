# Product Vision

## Vision

VIGIL helps people begin and sustain meaningful computer-based work by converting focus into a visible, calm and repeatable ritual.

## Product category

Desktop focus companion, not a generic task manager and not a historical combat game.

## Product promise

- Reduce the friction of starting.
- Keep one mission visible without creating another distraction.
- Make rest a deliberate part of the system.
- Preserve progress without punitive streak mechanics.
- Represent real work through a coherent Roman-inspired metaphor.

## Primary audience

Developers, designers, students, writers and independent professionals who work at a computer, understand what they need to do, but struggle with activation, task switching or continuity.

## Differentiation

The companion is not decoration placed on top of a timer. It is a peripheral state indicator tied to a disciplined work loop:

- **Ordo:** one active mission.
- **Vigilia:** a bounded watch/focus interval.
- **Disciplina:** consistent completed practice.
- **Campaign:** long-term project progress.
- **Debrief:** a small closure and next-action ritual.

These are product metaphors, not claims of exact historical simulation.

## Anti-vision

VIGIL must not become:

- a shame-based grindset product,
- a full project-management suite,
- an attention-demanding virtual pet,
- an unbounded AI chatbot,
- a gambling-like reward loop,
- a historical violence simulator.
